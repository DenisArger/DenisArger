# Projects Summary

## 1) telegram-event-registration-service
- Problem: Event registrations in Telegram chats are hard to track and operate.
- Solution: Multi-tenant SaaS with Telegram bot, admin panel, lifecycle actions, and CSV export.
- Result: Structured registration workflow with capacity/waitlist logic and auditable admin operations.

## 2) church-management-js
- Problem: Church communication and coordination workflows were fragmented across manual chats/processes.
- Solution: TypeScript Telegram platform integrated with Notion/Supabase and deployment scripts.
- Result: Centralized bot-driven operations with clearer structure and CI-backed repository quality.

## 3) work-reports
- Problem: Manual collection of work reports from Google Sheets/Drive was slow and inconsistent.
- Solution: Telegram bot with command-based aggregation and serverless webhook runtime.
- Result: Faster reporting cycles and repeatable report generation flow.

## 4) MyAI
- Problem: Telegram AI interactions need memory, media support, and stable backend integration.
- Solution: Next.js service integrating Telegram, OpenAI, and Supabase (chat history + storage).
- Result: Unified text/voice/media AI workflow with persistent context.

## 5) Track_parser
- Problem: Radio track preparation was manual and time-consuming.
- Solution: Next.js app for ingest, classification, processing, metadata handling, and upload.
- Result: Streamlined operator pipeline for audio preparation.

## 6) ControlWork
- Problem: Users need practical control over focused work sessions and break discipline.
- Solution: Cross-platform desktop app with tray controls, active-time tracking, and reminder modes.
- Result: Better routine support through local, low-friction productivity tooling.
